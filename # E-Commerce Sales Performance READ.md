\# **E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard**



\## **Overview**

This project presents an interactive E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI. The objective was to analyze overall sales performance, category trends, product efficiency, and customer review impact.



\## **Data Source**

\- Clean CSV dataset (E-Commerce sales data)

\- 12 months sales data

\- Product-level pricing and review metrics



\## **Tools Used**

\- Power BI

\- DAX (for calculated measures and columns)



\## **Dashboard Features**



\### **KPI Metrics**

\- Total Revenue: $6,000,000

\- Total Units Sold: 57,356

\- Average Revenue per Product: $6,000

\- Average Review Score: 3.03

\- Revenue per Review: 0.95



\### **Visualizations**

1\. Category-wise Revenue Analysis

2\. Revenue Percentage Contribution by Category

3\. Top 10 Products by Revenue

4\. Month-wise Revenue Trend (Line Chart)

5\. Price vs Review vs Revenue (Scatter Chart)



\### **Interactive Slicers**

\- Category Slicer

\- Price Range Slicer (Grouped in 50 USD bins)

\- Review Score Range Slicer (0–5 scale)



\## **Key Insights**

\- Books category is the highest revenue contributor.

\- Toys show strong and stable monthly performance.

\- Home \& Kitchen category underperforms compared to others.

\- Revenue concentration is higher among top-performing products.

\- Customer review score (3.03 avg) indicates room for service improvement.



\## **Business Recommendations**

1\. Continue investing in high-performing categories.

2\. Improve performance of low-revenue categories through promotions.

3\. Focus on customer satisfaction to increase review score.

4\. Replicate strategy of top-performing products.
